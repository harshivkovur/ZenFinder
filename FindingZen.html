Welcome to FindingZen

FindingZen caters to users' health and wellness by serving as a guide to better their well-being.

We strive to do this by tracking and monitoring patterns regarding your physical activity, sleep, nutrition, water, food, and menstrual habits.

Our mission is to provide users with insight on ways to foster personal growth through their daily lives.

Key Features to Track

How much time do you exercise each day?

How many hours of sleep do you get each night?:

Considered inadequate. Recommended to limit screen time, watch caffeine intake, follow a consistent sleep schedule, and evaluate stress levels.

Recommended amount. Ensure sleep is of quality by optimizing sleep environment, watch eating and intake habits, get regular exercise, limit screentime, and evaluate comfort.
Considered a higher range for sleep time. Ensure optimal sleeping habits and schedule and try to limit naps.

How many glasses of water do you drink every day?:

Considered inadequate. Women are typically recommended to 9 cups and men 13 cups to be properly hydrated. Drink more water: carry around a water bottle and refill it multiple times throughout the day.
Considered inadequate. Women are recommended to drink 9 cups and men 11 cups. To improve your water intake, carry around water bottles and set goals throughout the day.
Good water consumption. This is the recommended amount of water, so stay on this routine and continue your water habits.

Are you on your period?:

Being on your period can get tough sometimes, but here's some tips on how to get through it a little easier: stay hydrated, eat nutrition-rich foods, exercise if possible, take pain relievers, use warm compress, and make sure to get some rest!
Take this time to have some fun and practice self-care. Stay active and maintain a healthy lifestyle. Make sure to monitor your health as well.
